Frequently Asked Questions

Who can donate blood?

Generally, healthy individuals aged 18-65 who meet specific health criteria can donate blood.

How often can I donate?

Most people can donate whole blood every 56 days.

Is it safe to donate blood?

Yes, blood donation is safe when proper procedures are followed. All equipment is sterile and single-use.
